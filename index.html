ABOUT

I enjoy design, and I believe usability is the avoidance of user anxiety at each interaction. I am fascinated by Scandinavian style and enjoy simple natural materials and contrast.

In college, I rowed on the lightweight crew team, earning a Varsity Letter. Like many, I enjoy food and traveling. I was lucky to have had the opportunity to climb Mt. Kilimanjaro with my father. I am also an Eagle Scout.

I often hold unpopular opinions. I believe deeply in the importance of personal choice, and have long been fascinated with the concept of servant leadership. I enjoy both country music and techno.
