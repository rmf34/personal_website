After nearly 4.5 truly fantastic years of writing a variety of software for Salesforce.com, I decided to take a different direction and joined Citadel, LLC in Chicago. I am excited for the change and for the challenge and to get to know a different part of the country.

ABOUT

I grew up in Etna, New Hampshire, and attended Phillips Exeter before Cornell University. I have lived in New England, San Francisco, and New York City.

I enjoy design and believe usability is the avoidance of user anxiety at each interaction. I enjoy mid-century design and natural materials.

In college, I rowed on the lightweight crew team, earning a Varsity Letter. I enjoy cooking and baking. I was lucky to have had the opportunity to climb Mt. Kilimanjaro with my father. I am also an Eagle Scout.
