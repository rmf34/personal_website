ABOUT

I grew up in Etna, New Hampshire, and attended Phillips Exeter before Cornell University. I have lived in New England, New York City, and San Francisco.

While not a designer, I enjoy the consumption of design and believe usability is the avoidance of user anxiety at each interaction.

In college, I rowed on the lightweight crew team and earned a Varsity Letter. I enjoy trying new restaurants and at home cooking and baking. I was a distance runner 2012-2015 but am now focused on weight training. My golf game needs major work, and I am turning my lifelong passion for cars (read: engines) toward becoming an F1 enthusiast. I was lucky to have had the opportunity to walk up Mt. Kilimanjaro with my father. I am also an Eagle Scout.
